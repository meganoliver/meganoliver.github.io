About Megan

Hi, my name is Megan and I'm a Front End Web Developer living in Portland, OR. I was a middle school science teacher for 13 years and really enjoyed it for a while. Teaching is a stressful job and as I found keeping a work/family life balance more and more difficult, I decided that it was time for a big chnage!

I have been taking classes online through Treehouse for a couple of years. I enjoy the challenge and creativity of coding. When Treehouse started offering a Techdegree program, I jumped right on board. I am currently on a leave of absence from my teaching job so that I can give learning to code my full attention and hopefully turn it into a new career.
