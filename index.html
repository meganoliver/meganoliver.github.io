About Megan

Hi, my name is Megan and I'm a Full Stack, Front End Web Developer living in Portland, OR. I recently graduated from Treehouse with a Tech Degree in Front End Web Development and I am now working on my 2nd Tech Degree in Full Stack JavaScript while looking for full time employment. I am advanced in HTML, CSS, SASS, Git, JavaScript, and JQuery. I am also familiar with (and always improving on) Gulp, Node.js, React.js, and Express.

I really love web development. It's challenging and creative and I enjoy the fact that you really can't ever know everything, so learning is ongoing. I'm excited to find some full time work where I can get real world experience under my belt and learn from some veterans of the industry. I pride myself on being a creative thinker and an excellent team player. I firmly believe that 2(or 3 or 4) heads are always better than one.

While I'm applying for jobs, I'm working as a freelance developer, creating websites for some small businesses in my area (and one for my son, posted below!). I like that I can use my skill as a coder to help people take their businesses to the next level! If you are interested in having me build you a website, my email and phone number can be found in the "Contact" section of this page.
